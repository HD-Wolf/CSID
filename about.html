About

The purpose of this website is to introduce Computer Science students to important theoretical concepts, social justice issues, and best practices related to computer science. In doing so, I hope that this website will act as a starting point for students to think critically about computer science.

About Me

This website was created by Hana Darling-Wolf (me!), an undergraduate student in Computer Science and Gender, Sexuality, Feminist, and Social Justice Studies at McGill University. My research focuses on computer science identities and computer science education.

Although I am by no means an expert on these topics, I hope to make what knowledge I have accessible to other Computer Science students.
